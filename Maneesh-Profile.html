Biography

 
  • Maneesh Gona is currently studying at the Missouri University of Science and Technology. Maneesh had a difficult life growing up, due in part to his parents. His father is a South Indian from Kalkatta and his mother North Indian, which was a source of cultural discord. Maneesh has an undiagnosed psychological condition which makes it hard for him to understand people. His mother left his family when he was 6 years old and Maneesh assumed his father blamed him for it. This created distance between them and resulted in Maneesh alienating himself further by immersing himself in American pop-culture. His father allowed him to enroll at Rolla only so he could take classes which would help Maneesh one day take over his dad's falafel restaurant. Eventually Maneesh convinced his father to let him study Computer Science and Computer Engineering. During this time he grew close to a study group he joined, particularly Michael Schoen who became his best friend and roommate.
